About Us

Powering Nigeria's Clean Energy Future

Founded in 2018, SolarNaija is a Port Harcourt-based solar energy company committed to delivering reliable, affordable, and sustainable power solutions across Nigeria. We believe every Nigerian home and business deserves uninterrupted electricity.

From our humble beginnings with 5 residential installations, we've grown to become one of Rivers State's most trusted solar providers, with over 500 successful projects and a team of 40+ certified professionals.
